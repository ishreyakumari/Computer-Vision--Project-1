3. Color Models and Shadow Detection in Natural Images

Authors: Salvador, E., Cavallaro, A., & Ebrahimi, T. (2004)

Publication: IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems for Video Technology

Synopsis: Investigates the effectiveness of different color models (RGB, HSV, LAB, YUV) for shadow detection in natural images. Provides theoretical analysis and empirical validation of color-based shadow detection methods.

Key Findings:

  • LAB color space superiority for shadow detection
  • Statistical analysis of shadow properties
  • Adaptive thresholding techniques

This demonstration shows the step-by-step process of detecting and removing shadows from digital images. The algorithm uses a combination of color space analysis and morphological operations to achieve optimal results.

🛠️ Processing Pipeline

Step 1: Color Space Conversion

Convert the input RGB image to LAB color space for better luminance-chrominance separation.

Step 2: Shadow Detection

Apply adaptive thresholding on the L-channel to identify potential shadow regions.

Step 3: Morphological Refinement

Use opening and closing operations to refine shadow boundaries and remove noise.

Step 4: Shadow Removal

Apply illumination correction to the detected shadow regions while preserving texture details.
